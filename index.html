Cislunar Manufacturing

In-space and lunar-surface manufacturing — 3D printing, robotic assembly, metal forging, and advanced fabrication using local resources to produce structures, tools, parts, and habitats in cislunar space, dramatically reducing Earth launch costs.

Cislunar manufacturing enables scalable production for Artemis bases, orbital platforms, and future deep space missions, forming a key pillar of the self-sustaining lunar economy.
